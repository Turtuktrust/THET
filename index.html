Turtuk Health and Education Trust (THET)

REG NO: 28/343 of 2011

Introduction

The Turtuk Health and Education Trust (THET) was established on 28th April 2011. Since then, THET has consistently worked to improve health and education, particularly for the residents of Turtuk Youl and Turtuk Farol. Thousands of charitable donations have been made to poor and needy students and critically ill patients referred to hospitals in Leh and other states. THET has also arranged patient Sarais in Disket and Leh offering free facilities.

Funding and Incomes

THET has no permanent source of income. It collects annual membership fees ranging from Rs. 50 to Rs. 1000 from its trustees.

Achievements

Since its establishment, THET has supported hundreds of critical patients and students. Volunteers actively assist in patient care, coordinate with doctors, and manage medicines and boarding facilities. Students receive financial aid, stationery, uniforms, and school bags. Many have gone on to become doctors, engineers, teachers, and other professionals.

Mission

  • Provide financial assistance to poor and needy students.
  • Encourage academic excellence with scholarships and rewards.
  • Support students with stationery, bags, and uniforms.
  • Organize career counseling sessions.
  • Assist helpless patients with financial and medical support.
  • Conduct seminars, workshops, and public lectures.
  • Run hospitals, dispensaries, medical camps, and health centers.

Vision

  • Achieve 100% literacy in the block and adjoining areas.
  • Develop human resources for a better future.
  • Promote professionalism and skill development.
  • Make the area economically sound and self-sustaining.
  • Improve health and living standards.
  • Create a hygienic and disease-free society.
  • Build an intoxication-free society.
